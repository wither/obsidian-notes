- [ ] **VulnNet: Active** *(FREE)* - Multi-vector AD attacks

## Medium-Hard Difficulty

- [ ] **Persisting Active Directory** - Golden tickets + DCShadow + persistence

## Hard Difficulty

- [ ] **Holo** - Complex multi-domain scenarios
